Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

BrainAPI collects account contact details, API usage logs, and billing metadata required to operate and secure the service.

Data We Collect

  • Contact information you provide during onboarding or billing.
  • API key identifiers, request metadata, and usage analytics.
  • Payment metadata from Razorpay (no raw card numbers are stored by BrainAPI).

How We Use Data

  • Provide and secure API access.
  • Enforce rate limits, trial windows, and subscription status.
  • Detect abuse, fraud, and service misuse.

Data Sharing

We share data only with essential service providers such as hosting, observability, and payment processors.

Contact
