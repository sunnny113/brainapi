# Legal Review Checklist (India SaaS Launch)

Use this with your lawyer before public launch.

## Company & Contracting

- Confirm legal entity name, registered address, GST details (if applicable).
- Confirm governing law and jurisdiction clauses.
- Confirm contracting party displayed in Terms.

## Privacy Compliance

- Review data categories collected (name, email, API logs, billing metadata).
- Review purpose limitation and retention windows.
- Add DSAR/contact process and response SLAs.
- Confirm cross-border transfer language if using foreign cloud vendors.

## Terms of Service

- Define acceptable use and prohibited content clearly.
- Define account suspension/termination rights.
- Define liability limits, disclaimers, and uptime language.
- Define IP ownership for user inputs/outputs and service code.

## Billing & Refund Policy

- Confirm trial-to-paid transition wording.
- Confirm recurring billing consent language.
- Confirm cancellation and refund conditions.
- Confirm tax treatment and invoicing obligations.

## Razorpay & Payments

- Confirm payment processor disclosure text.
- Confirm webhook fraud/chargeback handling process.
- Confirm dispute and support escalation contacts.

## Security & Incident Response

- Add breach notification policy and timeline.
- Define support/security contact method.
- Document backup and recovery policy references.

## Public Pages To Lawyer-Review

- /ui/privacy.html
- /ui/terms.html
- /ui/refund.html

## Final Launch Sign-off

- Lawyer sign-off date
- Versioned policy snapshot archived
- Effective date updated on all legal pages
